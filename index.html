FIRE AWARENESS HOW TO PREVENT FIRE?
Fires may happen anytime, anywhere. Last 2022, there were 13, 029 recorded fire incidents here in the Philippines according to the Bureau of Fire Protection. Main causes of fire incidents here in the Philippines are usually: Faulty electrical connections, ciggarette butts and unattended stoves. By simply unplugging unused appliances lowers your risk for a fire as the cords may build up more power that may be directed to a flammable surface. Plus, unplugging an unused device may even save you more energy and extend that device's lifespan.
UNPLUG UNUSED APPLIANCES
Why is march considered the Philippines' Fire Prevention Month? Well it is because March is one of our warmest months and also the month where we are more prone to more fire incidents.(Fire Prevention Month Motorcade | Philippine Statistics Authority Region XI, 2023) When cooking, you must always keep an eye on the fire and make sure it is not to big of a fire. Additionally, you must always close close your stove when you are finished using it.
KEEP AN EYE ON YOUR STOVE
The Bureau of Fire Protection is incharge for fire incidents here in the Philippines. There are roughly about 600 stations nation wide, around 50 in the cities an 500 in the municiplaities. You can call 160/911 incase of emergencies. Having a fire extinguisher around secures having a safer environment for you to cook, for your appliances etc. and lessens your chances for starting a fire incident.
